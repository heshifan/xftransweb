# Contact Us

If you're working on website content, marketing materials, project documents, or arranging meetings, exhibitions, training, or other language support needs — send us your current situation. The sooner you clarify the use case, timeline, and target language, the smoother the assessment and execution will be.

## Information to Provide

To help us quickly determine the best service approach, you can prioritize providing the following:

- What is the material?
- What scenario is it for?
- What language pair is needed?
- When is it expected to be delivered or used?
- Are there terminology, format, confidentiality, or industry requirements?

For interpretation or simultaneous interpretation needs, please also specify the meeting time, location, number of participants, and format — this helps us determine the best support method.

## Services We Offer

- Translation services
- Interpretation services
- Simultaneous interpretation support
- Website and brand material localization
- Terminology management and language support

## How We Process Your Request

After receiving your requirements, we will first assess the content type, usage scenario, and timeline, then determine whether translation, interpretation, localization, or a combined approach is most suitable. This minimizes back-and-forth communication and makes subsequent arrangements clearer.

## Get Started

If you have clear requirements, you can send your materials or brief directly to us. We will first assess the appropriate approach based on your actual situation, then discuss next steps.
